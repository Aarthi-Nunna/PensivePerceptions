Pensive Perceptions

The sanest of thoughts would plead for imagination to go haywire

Words, the greatest power of them all.

A persons’ thoughts are drawn into a string of words that together form a poem, a paragraph, a piece of their mind, a thought they have fathomed that may just be able to pass of as a jumble of words to an unkeen eye.
The pictures that each phrase paints, with clauses that fit together to complete the puzzle, words truly have a unique power of its own.
Most people don’t pay heed to this power.
The power to humble a person, a means of expressing gratitude, a sharp knife in times of distress or means to a peace offering at dire times.
The power that words yield may not be acquired by drinking up a potion or finding a gemstone, it’s just a humble dance between imagination and the intellect of a human mind.
