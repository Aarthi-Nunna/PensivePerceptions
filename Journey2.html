Journey to SSN

A journey that will last a lifetime.

My journey started at Fairview International School Penang. I studied from Year 1 to Year 6 at this school, creating memories that I cherish everyday.
At Fairview, I had the privilige to study under the International Baccalaureate program. The program had taught me everything I know about application, research skills and creativity.
Despite a shortlived time at Fairview, I have made memories that will last for years to come as I had made friends from all over the world, learning and incorporating diversity and unity.

Fairview International School (FIS) is the premier International School in Malaysia that offers the whole array of IB curriculum.

From Class 6 to 12, I studied at Padma Seshadri Bala Bhavan Senior Secondary School, K K Nagar. A home away from home, filled with beuatiful memories, enriching learning and experiences.
With amazing teachers to coach us through the years, my Computer Science teacher truly stood out and incultated within me the passion towards Computer Science.

The PSBB KKN branched out of the main school in 1976 as part of the expansion programme,to meet the ever growing demans for admission.

At SSN, I started my journey in pursuing my Undergrad Degree in Computer Science Engineering.
A proud student of the 23' batch, I am currently in my third year at SSN. With amazing faculty, an encouraging peer group and a memory rich hostel life SSN has been my home away from home.

SSN College of Engineering established by Dr. Shiv Nadar, Founder Chairman of HCL Technologies, stand out as a premier center of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research.
